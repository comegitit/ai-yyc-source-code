DATA 440/480: Predictive Analytics

This course develops the business and analytical literacy needed to evaluate, interpret, and govern AI-driven predictive models in enterprise environments. The focus is on understanding how predictive models are built, validated, and applied to real-world business decisions. And critically, where they can fail.

Emphasis is placed on translating model outputs into actionable business insight, assessing model reliability and risk, and understanding the governance implications of deploying predictive AI in high-stakes decision-making contexts.

Course topics include:
  • Introduction to Predictive Analytics & Business Applications
  • Aligning Predictive Models with Business Goals — Case Study Approach
  • Data Quality, Preparation & Its Impact on Model Reliability
  • Model Development & the Role of Human Judgment
  • Model Evaluation, Risk Assessment & Communicating Uncertainty
Final Project:
  • An AI-powered Credit Risk Assessment Model supporting lending decisions — examining how predictive outputs inform high-stakes financial decisions, what governance guardrails are required, and where human oversight remains essential.
