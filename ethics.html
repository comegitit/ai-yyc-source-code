ETHI 401: AI Governance & Ethics

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of AI ethics and how they can be leveraged in AI system development and usage. Topics include AI development, principles of responsible AI, and policies and regulations concerning AI ethics in major jurisdictions. Using real-world case studies, students will also examine ethical concerns that may arise from using AI in organizations. Students be equipped to develop responsible and ethical AI systems, as well as advocate for AI ethics as users. The topics covered in this course are:

  • How Artificial Intelligence Works
  • Ethical Concerns in Artificial Intelligence
  • Responsible AI Principles and AI Governance
  • AI Policy and Regulation
  • Ethics in AI Development and Usage
