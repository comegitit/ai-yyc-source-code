ARTI 409: AI Management & Maintenance

This course focuses on the operational lifecycle of AI systems in enterprise environments, with emphasis on governance, oversight, and long-term sustainability. The objective is to understand how AI systems perform in production, how they are monitored and maintained, and how organizations ensure continued alignment with business objectives over time.

Particular attention is given to the governance challenges that emerge after deployment, including model drift, performance degradation, data integrity, and evolving regulatory requirements. The course examines how organizations establish controls, accountability structures, and risk management practices to ensure AI systems remain reliable, compliant, and ethically aligned in real-world operations.

Course topics include:
  • Ethical Oversight & Responsible AI in Production Systems
  • Data Governance, Quality Control & System Reliability
  • AI Compliance, Regulatory Considerations & Audit Readiness
  • AI System Lifecycle Management in Enterprise Environments
  • Performance Monitoring, Model Drift & Operational Risk
  • Cloud-Based AI Operations & Enterprise Infrastructure Considerations
Applied Focus:
  • Evaluation of AI systems in operational contexts, examining how organizations maintain performance, manage risk, and ensure alignment with business objectives and governance frameworks over time. Emphasis on identifying failure points, defining oversight mechanisms, and reinforcing accountability in production environments. (In progress – 2026)
